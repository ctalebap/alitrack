What is AliTrack?

AliTrack is a Chrome extension designed to automate the collection and tracking of AliExpress orders. The extension aggregates order details (product names, models, prices, order IDs, dates) and cross-references tracking information from individual tracking pages, presenting a consolidated table for easy analysis and export.

How It Works

  1. Navigate to your AliExpress Orders page
  2. Click "Collect All Orders Data" button in the extension popup
  3. Wait for auto-scroll to complete and table to render
  4. Open each "Track" link in new tabs to extract tracking numbers
  5. The extension automatically extracts and saves tracking numbers with collection dates
  6. Export your data to CSV or copy to clipboard for use in Excel, Google Sheets, or other tools

Use Cases

  • E-commerce resellers: Monitor multiple orders at once to manage customer fulfillment
  • Personal shoppers: Keep organized records of international purchases with tracking information
  • Logistics analysts: Aggregate shipping data for performance metrics and supplier evaluation
  • Data exporters: Maintain offline records of purchases for accounting, warranty, or archival purposes

Installation

Chrome Extension

Install AliTrack from the Chrome Web Store (or load as unpacked extension). Once installed, navigate to your AliExpress orders page to start using.

Requirements

  • Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari (with Tampermonkey or GreaseMonkey extension)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • AliExpress account (must be logged in to view your orders)
  • Active internet connection

Privacy & Security

  • No server uploads: All data remains on your machine in browser storage
  • No third-party tracking: Extension does not send data to external services
  • AliExpress only: Extension only runs on AliExpress domains and cannot access other websites
  • Manual control: You control when data is collected; no background automation sends data elsewhere
